## Where Dreams Can Come True [#where-dreams-can-come-true]

Imagination is an almost superhuman quality. Everything that mankind has accomplished throughout our history has come about thanks to our ability to imagine.

With imagination comes play, creativity, and the ability to see the world in new ways. All of the smartest people I've ever met allow themselves to move past "cringe" and think through ideas without judging others or themselves for them.

They hold the idea in their mind and examine it with childlike wonder, much like someone would inspect a smooth, colorful stone. They add to the idea, polish it, and brainstorm new ways with others to change their world.
